Project Description

Equitoria raises money by supporting businesses to grow, earning income as a shareholder in those businesses.


Businesses don't have to have Equitoria as a shareholder, but if they choose to have Equitoria invest into their business or support the business in other ways then it's in exchange for shares and it's possession of those shares that builds wealth and revenue flows across the economy and into the state treasury.


This means the state doesn't tax businesses while providing no tangible value back but rather the state acts as a partner, providing value to the business in exchange for value back.
