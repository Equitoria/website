Memecoin Description

Memes are ideas, often expressed as images, that become shared by people who have a belief in that meme.


Therefore, popular memes are indicators of what people really think and feel and are a powerful way to peek into the mind of society to see what would otherwise go unsaid.


Memecoins are a web3 way of formalising memes by creating tokens representing a meme that people buy and hold as a way of showing affiliation with the message in the meme.

Equitoria recognises this modern phenomena for expression through memecoins and so will monitor and support memes as a way of expressing ideas and views that the state via the citizens can help to formulate policy.
