Equitoria the place, Description

It is planned for Equitoria to buy land such as a large island or other region with the express understanding that this place will declare independence and seek state recognition.


This can only happen when the economy exists to support the acquisition and running of a state, when there are sufficient citizens to form a state and sufficient businesses to found a state.


To begin, we will form a business centre to create revenue and jobs and to support the relocation of citizens who choose to be early pioneers of this new state.


This state will be located near to a large trading hub such as Asia, Europe of America to facilitate trade and early growth.


The early objective will be to grow jobs and businesses and to bring more people to the state, building a thriving citizen led economy.


In the more distant future, through success of the state we hope to see other states apply to join us to grow a larger citizen run commonwealth as well as encouraging countries that remain independent to recognise the benefit of supporting and enriching their citizens, sharing the benefit globally.
