Digital First Economy Description

Equitoria aims to be a digital first economy which we define as being a country built on new technologies, leveraging technology for the benefit of the states citizens and businesses.


We want to build new web3, DeFi, AI and other online businesses that can scale to unicorn status, providing a high quality citizen experience with citizens learning highly desirable skills.
