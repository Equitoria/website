Shares Wealth Description

Equitoria is owned and run by its citizens and so its incentivised to share wealth and success broadly.


For example, if the state treasury builds up excessive levels of wealth then the state should pay out a dividend to citizens. That's right, a state that doesn't take away the wealth of its citizens through taxes but a state that gives wealth out to its citizens.
