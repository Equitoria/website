HOW ABOUT SOME Fun Facts

Switzerland allows its citizens to propose initiatives to create new laws, challenge existing laws, and vote on constitutional amendments.

Some Swiss Cantons practiced direct democratic governance in local assemblies, where citizens would gather and vote on laws and policies.

Colonial New England (17th to 18th century), especially in Massachusetts and Connecticut, some towns used town meetings for direct democratic governance.

Ancient Athens (5th to 4th century BCE), citizens participated directly in the Ecclesia, the main governing assembly, proposing, debating and voting on laws.

Community skills

Equitoria is community owned and led. Whoever you are, you can help to build this new country. We believe that a strong country is run by its citizens, is tolerant of diversity across its citizenship, and exists to build up and help citizens to succeed in all that they set out to achieve. We don't extract value from citizens through taxes, we partner with citizens to build businesses that thrive and give money back to the state as a partner and investor in its success.
