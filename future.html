Future Description

Equitoria's future will be decided by the citizens of Equitoira, but it is hoped that we will see some foundational features emerge.

  • That people will once more take an interest in governance when they start to have a say in matters of governance.
  • That we will see a return to family values and communities working together without fear of dictats from the state.
  • That the state will avoid becoming a debt based economy which is a plague on other economies around the world.
  • That the state will be economically successful and pass that success on to its citizens.
  • That businesses will thrive and a new age of innovation and growth will become the hallmark of Equitora.
  • That we will see an end to corruption and sleaze in government.
  • That success will radiate outwards and encourage other states to join us or to become more democratic and postive for their citizens.
  • That we will within 20 years begin building our physical city state near to a high trade region in the world.
