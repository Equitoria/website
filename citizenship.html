Citizenship Description

Equitoria aims some day to have a physical city state where citizens can move to for the jobs and full benefits provided by the state.


That means provision of all the rights and protections of citizenship along with a passport for international travel.


Even before having a physical location we intend to issue a passport and to test its recognition in multiple countries around the world.
